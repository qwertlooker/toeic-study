Weston Foods to Acquire Pablo Cafe Chain
Weston Foods announced yesterday that it will acquire Pablo, a regional cafe chain with 36 locations across South America. According to the company, the acquisition will allow Weston to expand its prepared-food business and enter several new urban markets within the next year.
Pablo will continue operating under its current brand name during the transition. Weston stated that no store closures are planned at this time and that customers can expect the same menu while management teams work together on future product development.
