Notice: Bulletin Board Space for Local Groups
Beginning June 1, community organizations may reserve bulletin board space in the Riverside Library lobby. The space may be used to post announcements about educational programs, volunteer opportunities, and noncommercial events that are open to the public.
All materials must be approved by library staff before posting and must be removed within 14 days. Items promoting private businesses or political campaigns will not be accepted. For reservation requests, complete a short form at the front desk.
